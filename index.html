About Us

Welcome to SMS Team International Limited, a market-leading manufacturer of labels, packaging, and accessories with a proud heritage dating back to 1991. Based in south China, we are renowned for our expertise in branded packaging trims and supply chain solutions,
delivering exceptional value to our customers.

At SMS Team, we are passionate about providing comprehensive, creative, and tailor-made concepts in branding, design, product development, and logistics solutions. Our team of highly skilled professionals is dedicated to working closely with our clients to deliver customized solutions that meet their unique needs and exceed their expectations. We take pride in our cutting-edge technology and state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities, which enable us to produce high-quality products quickly and efficiently. Our commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction has earned us a reputation for excellence in the industry, and we are proud to be one of the largest companies in our field. Thank you for considering SMS Team International Limited for your label, packaging, and accessory needs. We look forward to working with you and helping to take your business to the next level.
